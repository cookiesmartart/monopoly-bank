Monopoly Bank replaces the paper money in Monopoly board game nights with a simple, fully local
digital banker for 2-8 players.

This app was (largely) built with the help of AI (Claude Code). See the project's README for
details.

Features:
* Fully offline — no network access, no accounts, no analytics, nothing leaves your device
* Manual banking: pay the bank, receive from the bank, or transfer between players, with a
  quick-entry keypad and shortcuts for common amounts
* Negative balances are allowed (with a clear warning), matching the real rules — a player can
  go into debt until they sell off property
* Optional NFC: link a blank NFC sticker to a player, then tap to identify players and confirm a
  payment. Manual entry always works too, with or without NFC hardware
* Transaction history, per player and globally
* Light and dark mode, with optional sound/vibration feedback
* English, Dutch, and Spanish included

Single device, pass-and-play — all players share one phone, no accounts or syncing needed.
